Math for Games

Category Function/Concept Description
Geometry distance() Calculates the distance between two points.
Geometry angleBetween() Calculates the angle between two vectors.
Geometry intersect() Determines if two shapes intersect.
Trigonometry sin() Calculates the sine of an angle.
Trigonometry cos() Calculates the cosine of an angle.
Trigonometry tan() Calculates the tangent of an angle.
Vectors add() Adds two vectors.
Vectors subtract() Subtracts one vector from another.
Vectors dotProduct() Calculates the dot product of two vectors.
Vectors crossProduct() Calculates the cross product of two vectors.
Physics velocity() Calculates the velocity of an object.
Physics acceleration() Calculates the acceleration of an object.
Physics force() Calculates the force applied to an object.
Physics momentum() Calculates the momentum of an object.
Physics collision() Determines if two objects collide.
Randomness random() Generates random numbers.
Randomness noise() Generates Perlin noise values.
Transformations translate() Moves the origin of the canvas to a different location.
Transformations rotate() Rotates the canvas around the origin.
Transformations scale() Increases or decreases the size of shapes drawn to the canvas.
